    Privacy & safety

    When enabled, every ChatGPT conversation you open is parsed in the background so it's ready when you open the popup. Turn this off to only capture when you click Refresh.
    • ChatGPT conversations are parsed locally on this device. When auto-capture is on, parsing happens as soon as the conversation tab finishes loading; when off, nothing is read until you open this popup and click Refresh.
    • Search pages (Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo) are captured only when you open this popup on that tab — the extension has no standing access to search domains.
    • Stored locally on this device: the latest ChatGPT conversation (prompts, fan-out queries, cited URLs) and the latest SERP snapshot.
    • Sends nothing off-device. No analytics. No cloud sync.

    Note: “Search origin” is inferred from visible search queries, citations, and saved conversation metadata. Some live tool calls may not be serialized into the payload, so hidden retrieval steps can still be missed.

    Google Mode A: Search capture is heuristic and may miss or misclassify results if Google changes page markup.
